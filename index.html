Our Company

Caduceus Solutions is a medical telecommunications company that strives to create secure web clients for virtual medical appointments and visits. With assistance from doctors around the world, we at Caduceus Solutions have been able to connect specialists to over 5,000,000 patients around the globe with The Sanctuary™. Patients only have to fill a short form and with help from our AI cognitive assistant, they’ll be connected to their doctor in almost an instant. The Sanctuary™ takes care of appointments, customer service, and analytics.

Our Product

The Sanctuary™ is a revolutionary new health-focused web client, designed for the sole purpose of virtual doctor visits. We implemented advanced end-to-end encryption on the software, all with the purpose of ensuring a safe way to have a secure visit with your doctor. The Sanctuary allows for conversations, files, records, and any other private matters relevant to the doctor's visit to stay secure and safe, so you can focus on your health.

Our Vision

Our vision to simplify the medical sector by reducing wait times for non-emergency appointments, so people can still get the care they need in a fast, easy, and professional way.
